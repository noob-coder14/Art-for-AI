Project - Art.ificial Verse 1

We have a limited collection of 999 artwork which is the result of human-AI collaboration.

Human input, a neural generative model (AI), and a procedural generative approach have come together for organic transformations called Art.ificial Verse (AiVerse).

Tech Details

In short, the Ai will generate an image and the Ai2 will measure how well the image matches the image. Then, the Ai1 uses the feedback from the Ai2 model to generate more “accurate” images.

This iteration will be done many times until the score becomes high enough and the generated image matches the text.
